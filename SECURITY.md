# Khora Security Policy

## Responsible Disclosure
Khora is committed to maintaining the security and integrity of its software. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly by contacting the author at [contact Discord : 2b53]. Do **not** publicly disclose the vulnerability until it has been addressed.

## Scope
This policy applies to:
- All code included in the Khora framework
- Modules, scripts, and any example payloads provided
- Dependencies maintained by the Khora project team

## Reporting Guidelines
When reporting a vulnerability, please include:
- A detailed description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce the vulnerability
- Potential impact and risk assessment
- Any relevant logs, screenshots, or proof-of-concept code

## Response Timeline
The Khora team will acknowledge your report within 7 days and provide updates on the status of the issue. Critical vulnerabilities will be addressed as quickly as possible.

## Safe Harbor
By reporting a vulnerability in good faith, you will not face legal or administrative action for your discovery, provided that:
- You do not exploit the vulnerability outside a controlled environment
- You avoid impacting production systems without explicit permission
- You report the issue only to the Khora team until it is resolved

## Disclaimer
Khora is designed for authorized security testing and educational purposes only. Misuse of the framework on unauthorized systems is strictly prohibited and may violate applicable laws.
